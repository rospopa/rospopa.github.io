  • Residential Property Calculator
    Determine the financial viability of potential investments with this all-in-one tool. Input property values, loan profiles, and market rates to generate a comprehensive overview of your acquisition costs and long-term equity growth. Gain a clear understanding of your cash flow by tracking recurring costs like taxes, insurance, and utilities while analyzing the income potential of multi-unit properties. This comprehensive calculator provides an instant snapshot of your property's health with automated metrics for Cap Rate, ROI, and Cash on Cash Return, alongside interactive charts for loan amortization and cumulative wealth building.
  • Exchange Deadline Calculator
    Two distinct deadlines drive the 1031 exchange process, the 45-day identification period and the 180-day exchange period. Use this deadline calculator to understanding your potential 1031 exchange timeline.
  • Depreciation Calculator
    Use this simple depreciation calculator to estimate how much your property may depreciate over time. Just enter a few details to get a quick snapshot of potential tax deductions, value reduction, and accumulated depreciation over the duration of ownership.
  • Capital Gains Calculation
    This capital gains tax calculator will give you a preliminary estimate of the potential tax liability on your real estate transaction and provide a comparison of the figures with and without a 1031 exchange.
  • Debt Replacement Calculator
    To achieve full tax deferral in a 1031 exchange, a general guideline is that the Exchanger must “trade up or stay even” in value. This calculator is designed to help estimate the total debt replacement needed to meet that requirement when reinvesting in a passive real estate investment.
