Brief on Marcus Aurelius' Meditations

2019

“Everything we hear is an opinion, not a fact. Everything we see is a perspective, not the truth.” ¹

Truth, it cannot be seen or touched, tasted or smelled, breathed or eaten. Truth, the ultimate form of information when

the state of affairs between relative (interdependent) concepts seems stable. The illusion of certainty is "truth".

Why can't we know anything for certain?

Certainty dissolves power of will. Once there potential for events to be determined (certain/True) then beings participating

in the unraveling nature (experiencing) of such events cannot influence the outcome despite having influence on any multitude

of factors because albeit all is interdependent, everything holds it's own weight, and thus there is varying impact of influence.

Does power of will mean I have power over my environment?

No. "You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength" ²
